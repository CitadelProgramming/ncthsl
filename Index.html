Ownership

Nigeria Customs Service (NCS) and the Ministry of Finance Incorporated (MOFI)

The NCT&HSL was established in August 2016 as a private company limited by shares pursuant to the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990, with the NCS and the MOFI as subscribers to the Memorandum of Association.

About Us

NCT&HSL is a premier provider of aviation services, renowned for its comprehensive range of offerings, technical expertise, and commitment to excellence in service delivery. Our aviation services are designed to meet the diverse needs of clientele, ensuring safety, efficiency, and satisfaction at every turn.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission

To deliver world-class aircraft maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) services with integrity, precision, and innovation that exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading hub for aviation technical services, safety assurance, and hangar operations, recognized globally for excellence.

Our Core Values

Safety • Excellence • Integrity • Innovation • Teamwork

Our Services

  • Fixed Based Operations (FBO)

    Our FBO services are tailored to provide seamless support for private, corporate, and general aviation aircraft.

  • Fueling, Parking, & Lounging

    We provide reliable and efficient fueling services, utilizing high quality aviation fuel to meet your aircraft's needs.

  • Sales and Leasing of Aircraft

    NCT&HSL offers comprehensive sales and leasing solutions for a variety of aircraft.

  • Aircraft Charter

    For those seeking flexibility and convenience, our aircraft charter services offer personalized travel solutions.

  • Helicopter & fixed wing aircraft services

    Our aviation services for oil and gas operators are engineered to meet the demanding requirements of the industry.

  • Aviation Consultancy

    Our aviation consultancy services leverage our extensive industry knowledge and expertise to provide strategic advice and solutions.

Our Capabilities

Aircraft Maintenance

Comprehensive maintenance, inspection, and repair services to ensure airworthiness and compliance with aviation standards.

Engineering Support

Providing technical and engineering assistance to improve operational efficiency and aircraft system reliability.

Hangar Operations

Modern hangar facilities for storage, inspections, and line maintenance for both light and heavy aircraft types.

Safety & Compliance

We operate under strict adherence to the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority (NCAA) and global MRO standards. Our certified engineers and technicians follow approved maintenance procedures to ensure safety, reliability, and compliance in every operation.
